While installing a garbage disposal can be a DIY project for those familiar with plumbing and electrical work, hiring a professional ensures it is done safely and correctly.

Avoid putting grease, fibrous vegetables (like celery), non-food items, hard materials (such as bones), and starchy foods (like potato peels) in the disposal to prevent clogs and damage.
